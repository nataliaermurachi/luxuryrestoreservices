About us

We are a full-service provider of french polishing and magic repair services. Our services include everything from traditional french polishing to spray finishes, magic repairs on a wide range of surfaces and more. Luxury Restore Services LTD comprises of highly qualified skilled workers with extensive work experience and we pride ourselves on providing excellent service along with a very high standard of work.

We combine traditional french polishing techniques with the best of modern technology and practices all underpinned by a quality management policy to ensure our work always meets the highest standards possible. We aim to meet and exceed customer expectation at prices which represent the best value for money.
